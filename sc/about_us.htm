Main Unit

The main unit of the Students' Club is located near Georgia and Punjab National Bank. It has various facilities for students to avail such as Snooker , Pool , Table Tennis , Carrom , Foosball , Chess and Air Hockey. Starting from the 2018-19 session , this unit will also have gaming consoles for the students. Other than the games , the main unit has a TV room , open wifi , music system and the students' square for hosting different events.

Timings
10:00 AM to 12:00 PM

Alaknanda Unit

The Alaknanda unit is located in the Multi Activity Centre (MAC) . It is is situated very close to 5 Bhawans making it easily accessible for a large number of students. It has facilities such as Snooker , Pool , Table Tennis , Carrom , Chess , Air Hockey and Foosball. It also has a TV room , open wifi and a music system.

Timings
10:00 AM to 10:00 PM

Jawahar Unit

Timings
